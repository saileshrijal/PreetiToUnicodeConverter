Why Convert Preeti Font to Unicode?

Preeti font is a proprietary font, which means that it is owned by a particular company and can only be used with their permission. This makes it difficult to use Preeti font on digital platforms, such as websites and mobile devices, where it may not be supported. Unicode, on the other hand, is an open-source encoding system that supports a vast range of characters, including those used in the Nepali language. This makes Unicode the preferred choice for representing Nepali text online, as it ensures that the text will be correctly displayed on a wide range of digital platforms.
